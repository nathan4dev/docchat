# PulseGrid Pricing and Plans

PulseGrid, AuroraByte's observability product, is sold under three subscription tiers.

**Starter** costs $99 per month and supports up to 10 monitored services, 7 days of data retention, and email-only support. It is intended for small teams and side projects.

**Growth** costs $499 per month and supports up to 100 monitored services, 30 days of data retention, custom alerting rules, and a shared Slack channel with AuroraByte's support team.

**Enterprise** is custom-priced and includes unlimited monitored services, 1 year of data retention, single sign-on, a dedicated customer success engineer, and a guaranteed 1-hour response time for critical incidents.

All plans include a 14-day free trial with no credit card required. Customers can upgrade or downgrade at any time, and unused trial days do not roll over.

PulseGrid does not charge for ingesting historical data during onboarding, but historical backfills are capped at 90 days regardless of plan tier.
